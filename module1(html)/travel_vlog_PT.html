MY TRAVEL VLOG

I recently took a short trip to Cox’s Bazar, and it was an unforgettable experience. Walking along the world’s longest sea beach, feeling the cool breeze and watching the sunset over the Bay of Bengal was incredibly relaxing. The sound of the waves and the lively local food stalls made the evenings special. It was a perfect escape from daily routine and left me refreshed and happy.
